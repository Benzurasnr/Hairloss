Frequently Asked Questions

What causes hair loss in men?

Hair loss in men is often caused by genetics (androgenetic alopecia), hormonal changes, stress, or medical conditions. Understanding the underlying cause is key to finding the right treatment.

Are there natural remedies for hair loss?

Yes, some natural remedies, like essential oils, scalp massages, and maintaining a balanced diet, can support hair health. However, results may vary based on the cause of hair loss.

How long does it take to see results from treatments?

The time varies depending on the treatment. Medications like Minoxidil may take several months, while surgical options can show immediate results. Consistency is crucial.
